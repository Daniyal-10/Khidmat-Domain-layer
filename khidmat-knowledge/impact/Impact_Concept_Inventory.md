# Impact Concept Inventory

## Core Concepts
1. **Outcome Indicator**: A specific, measurable characteristic used to assess change.
2. **Baseline**: The initial measurement of an indicator before intervention.
3. **Endline**: The final measurement of an indicator after intervention.
4. **Milestone Measurement**: Interim measurements taken during an ongoing intervention.
5. **Impact Score/Rating**: A qualitative or quantitative assessment of the intervention's success.
6. **Theory of Change**: The underlying logic of how a specific intervention is expected to produce a specific outcome.

## Sub-Concepts (Operational)
- **Survey Instrument**: The questionnaire or tool used to collect impact data.
- **Measurement Cohort**: A group of beneficiaries tracked together for impact analysis.
- **Counterfactual (Conceptual)**: What would have happened without the intervention (rarely measured directly, but contextually relevant for high-level evaluation).
