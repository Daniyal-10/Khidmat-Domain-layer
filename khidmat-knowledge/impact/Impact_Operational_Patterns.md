# Impact Operational Patterns

## Common Execution Patterns

1. **The Pre-Post Pattern**: Standard baseline measurement at enrollment, followed by endline measurement at case closure.
2. **The Longitudinal Pattern**: Periodic follow-up surveys (e.g., 3, 6, 12 months) triggered automatically after support delivery is completed.
3. **The Multi-Indicator Pattern**: Evaluating a single intervention across multiple dimensions (e.g., evaluating a cash transfer on both food security and psychological wellbeing).
4. **The Exception/Negative Pattern**: Immediate escalation pattern when an impact assessment reveals a worsening condition, triggering a new Needs Assessment.
5. **The Cohort Evaluation Pattern**: Aggregating individual impact scores across a specific demographic or geographic area to evaluate systemic program effectiveness.
