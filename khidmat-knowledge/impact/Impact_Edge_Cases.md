# Impact Edge Cases

## Complex Scenarios to Model

1. **Ghost Beneficiaries (Lost to Follow-up)**: Beneficiary cannot be located for endline measurement. How is the impact recorded? (e.g., `Inconclusive`).
2. **Conflicting Impact Reports**: A field volunteer observes improvement (e.g., shelter is fixed), but the beneficiary reports feeling less safe.
3. **Intervention Interruption**: A case is suspended halfway through delivery. How is the partial impact measured?
4. **Catastrophic External Events**: A successful livelihood intervention is wiped out by a subsequent flood before the endline measurement.
5. **Proxy Reporting on Impact**: When a guardian reports on the impact of an intervention on a child or dependent, capturing the proxy nature of the feedback.
