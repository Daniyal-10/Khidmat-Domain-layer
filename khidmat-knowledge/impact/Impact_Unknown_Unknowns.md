# Impact Unknown Unknowns

## Areas Requiring Further Investigation

1. **Attribution vs. Contribution**: How do we measure whether Khidmat's intervention *caused* the impact, vs. external factors (e.g., macroeconomic improvement, other NGOs)?
2. **Negative Impact / Do No Harm**: How systematically can the domain capture unintended negative consequences of an intervention?
3. **Long-Term Engagement**: How do we maintain contact with beneficiaries 6-12 months post-intervention to measure sustained impact without violating privacy or raising false expectations of further aid?
4. **Community vs. Individual Impact**: If we provide community-level support (e.g., repairing a well), how do we model the aggregated impact on the individual households?
5. **Standardization of Indicators**: Should Khidmat adopt global standards (like sphere standards or UN SDGs) directly into the taxonomy, or use a localized indicator framework?
