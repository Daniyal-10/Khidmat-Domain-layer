# Impact Business Flow Validation Report

## Overview
Validates the operational workflows within the Impact domain against humanitarian realities.

## Validated Flows
1. **Post-Intervention Baseline Establishment**: Triggered when a case plan is initiated, establishing the starting metrics.
2. **Outcome Measurement Collection**: Triggered at defined intervals (e.g., 3 months post-delivery) to collect endline data.
3. **Longitudinal Tracking**: Periodic follow-ups to measure sustained impact beyond immediate case closure.
4. **Program Evaluation Feedback Loop**: Aggregating individual impact measurements into program-level evaluations.

## Humanitarian Realities Addressed
- **Beneficiary Fatigue**: Over-surveying beneficiaries can cause harm or annoyance. Flow must include strict limits on survey frequency.
- **Subjectivity of Impact**: Recognizing that self-reported wellbeing improvements may differ from objective metrics, and both must be captured.
- **Deterioration Despite Intervention**: The flow must handle cases where impact is negative (e.g., condition worsened), triggering potential safety escalations or new case creation.
